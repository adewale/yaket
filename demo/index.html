Yaket demo

Compare Yaket and TF-IDF on the same text.

Yaket is a TypeScript port of YAKE for Single-document keyword extraction. It works with No training corpus and uses Local statistical features such as frequency, position, spread, casing, and co-occurrence. Lower score means more relevant.
